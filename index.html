Past Research

Girasek, Q.L., Schields B., Buonaccorsi V.P., Matter J.M. 2021. Phylogenetic Identification of North American Ratsnakes (Pantherophis sp.) in Central Pennsylvania, USA. Herpetological Review. 52: 17-20

We performed a phylogenetic analysis using cytochrome b gene sequences to make species assignments and to determine genetic affinities of snakes in Huntingdon, PA.
